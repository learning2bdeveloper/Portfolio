GIRING - Tool for Start-ups

Capstone Web & Mobile Project

Web Platform (Admin Access):
Exclusively accessible by the owner of the startup.
The owner manages administrative tasks, account creation, and system settings.

Mobile App (Employee Access):
Used by employees.
Employees track and input expenses and stocks.
Employees can view their payroll.

Key Functions:

  • Employee Tracking
  • Payroll with 2023 Tax Reduction
  • Bill Payment Reminder
  • Budgeting
  • Invoicing
  • Inventory
  • Expenditure Management
  • Analytics

Tech stack: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, Android Studio (Java)
